BRCGS AUDIT READINESS — Africa to EU Export Notes:
- Most African food manufacturers require a BRCGS audit for UK retailer access
- Certification is valid for 12 months (announced or unannounced)
- Grade: AA+ (>95%), AA (95%), A (90%), B (80%), C (70%), D (<70%)
- Unannounced audits now mandatory from Issue 9
